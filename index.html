Frequently Asked Questions

How to get information about vaccines?

You can search about the vaccine on the search bar and then the appropriate vaccines will be displayed according to your requirements. You can find the consultation icon on the rightmost corner of the webpage.

What if we have allergies?

We have provided a facility of consulting a Pediatrician in case of any allergies, side effects and etc.
You can find the option of consultation at the top right most corner of the website.

Where do we take the vaccines from?

Location of our nearby hospitals will be displayed in the map provided in our website, from there we can select any hospital for further booking of slots.
